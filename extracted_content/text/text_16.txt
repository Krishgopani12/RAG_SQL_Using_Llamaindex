Toronto's 3 distinct Chinatown is one of many places where immigrants live and their culture is kept alive.

Niagara Falls is a popular tourist attraction not far from Toronto.

Toronto City Hall, where the city government meets, is on the left. On the right is Old City Hall, which is now an appeals court.

There are many parks in the city. One of the big ones, just west of downtown, is called High Park.

The St. Lawrence Market is a busy indoor market. Many people buy fresh produce, meat, and seafood here.

In Scarborough, a suburb of Toronto, there is the Toronto Zoo, where many animals are taken care of.

There are many museums in Toronto, such as the Royal Ontario Museum.

Yorkdale Shopping Centre is just one of many large shopping malls outside downtown Toronto.
